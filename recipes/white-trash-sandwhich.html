White Trash Sandwich

This one is pretty simple

Ingredients

  • Bread... doesn't matter the type
  • Imagination

Directions

  • Place 2 slices of bread on a plate.
  • Take 1 separate piece of bread and put it in the toaster.
  • When cooked to preferred doneness remove from toaster.
  • Place toasted bread in-between untoasted pieces of bread.
  • ENJOY!
