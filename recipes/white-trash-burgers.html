White Trash Burgers!

While the image above is a little misleading, as we'll be using Hawaiian rolls as buns, this will be White Trash Burgers!

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp of Butter
  • Pack of Hawaiian rolls
  • 1lb of ground chuck
  • 1 Onion

Directions

  1. Mold ground chuck into a patty. (small enough to fit in Hawaiian rolls)
  2. Season patties with salt and pepper on both sides.
  3. Chop onion into rings, then cut rings in half.
  4. Heat frying pan over medium heat
  5. Melt Butter
  6. Add chopped onions to heat
  7. Once onions have gotten soft, time to add the patties.
  8. Make sure to have a sear on both sides and cooked through, reaching internal temp of 160 degrees.
  9. Assemble the burger and enjoy!
