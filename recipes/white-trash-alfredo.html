First off, White Trash Alfredo

Being the fanciest of the recipes today, we will start here.

Ingredients needed

  • 3 Tbsp of Butter
  • 8 fluid ounces of heavy whipping cream
  • 1 pinch of ground nutmeg
  • 2/4 cup of grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg yolk
  • hand full of fettuccine pasta

Directions

  1. Melt 3 Tbsp of butter over medium heat.
  2. Add heavy cream. (while stirring constantly)
  3. Stir in salt, pepper, nutmeg, and grated parmesan cheese.
  4. Once sauce is to the thickness you like, add the egg yolk and stir.

You can be all "fancy shmancy take a trip to Francy" and add cooked chicken, but it is not advised. Dad is gonna have to steal more copper for that chicken.
